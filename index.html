About Zanaya Trips

Discover the Passion Behind Every Journey

Your Trusted Partner for Memorable Travel Experiences
Zanaya Trips - Travels & Holidays LLP is a premium travel brand based in Cochin, Kerala, specializing in private and customized international holiday experiences.
We are proudly backed by a UAE-based travel company with over 9+ years of experience in the travel and tourism industry, serving clients across the globe.
At Zanaya Trips - Travels & Holidays LLP, we focus on creating personalized journeys that match your travel style, comfort, and expectations — ensuring every trip is smooth, memorable, and truly premium.
